# Privacy Policy for Task And Ticket Tracker

**Last Updated:** June 7, 2026

## 1. Data Collection
Task And Ticket Tracker ("the App") is a local, client-side application. The App does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal information, telemetry, or user data with the developer or any third parties.

## 2. Local Storage
All tasks, tickets, and settings created within the App are stored entirely locally on your device's hard drive. No data ever leaves your computer.

## 3. Contact
If you have any questions or concerns regarding this privacy policy, please contact the developer via the GitHub repository issue tracker.
